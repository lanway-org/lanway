How it works

Free in three steps

From zero to protected in minutes. One person sets up a server, everyone they share with browses freely.

01

Deploy a server

One click with the Manager app, or one command on any VPS. A private node spins up in under a minute — no domain required.

02

Share a key

Generate an access key with a data limit, then share the QR code or link. Friends and family scan it once.

03

Connect & browse

Open the Lanway app, tap connect, and the open internet is back. No throttling, no blocks, no limits.

Why Lanway

Built to be unblockable

Most VPNs get detected and throttled. Lanway hides in plain sight as everyday web traffic.

Unblockable

REALITY & TLS make your connection look identical to normal HTTPS — censorship filters can't tell it apart.

Free forever

Open source, no subscriptions, no paywalls. Run it yourself — it costs only your own server, if anything.

Easy setup

One-click cloud deploy or a single install command. The Manager app handles users, keys and limits for you.

Private

No logs, no accounts, no tracking. There's no Lanway company server — your data never leaves your own node.
